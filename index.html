ENGLISH POETRY

They say love dies after marriage
Huhh I never believed these words and will never believe. You know why?
Cuz I've seen it when my mum gets annoyed and starts shouting but my father don't even say a word to her and even try to make her calm.
I've seen it when my chachi wasn't well and my chachu cooked food for his family even after a full day office routine.
I saw it today when my chachi came out of her room got ready for a friend's wedding and my chachu looked at her, smiled gently and she smiled back to him I saw love even after 12 years of marriage in those sweet smiles...
And when she said she's going alone as chachu was just back home from office so she didn't want to disturb him she said she'll go with the driver and asked him if he wanna come I'l saw love in that jealous tone when he said back " Nahi tum jao akeli" and she laughed.
I don't believe these words when I see my phupo finding his husband in the whole house calling him by putting "mere" before his name...
When she came to me and said " laibu tmne mere abdullah ko dekha ha"
I never believed those words when I saw my father adoring my mother even at this age of life after 24 years of marriage.
I'm thankful to GOD that I have reasons not to believe these words and I just pray that I never get chance to believe this words Ameen!
